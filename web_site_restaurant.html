Maad

Maad juice is a traditional Senegalese drink made from the fruit of the maad tree. It features a mildly sweet and slightly tangy flavor, making it a popular local choice for staying hydrated and enjoying the taste of Senegal.

Mango

Mango juice is a tropical delight, offering the pure, sweet essence of ripe mangoes. It's a beloved choice for a thirst-quenching beverage, particularly when mangoes are in season.

Ditakh

Ditakh juice is a refreshing Senegalese beverage made from the fruit of the ditakh tree, also known as the snowball tree. It offers a unique blend of sweet and tangy flavors, making it a popular choice for quenching your thirst on a hot day.

Bouye

Bouye juice is derived from the baobab fruit, a native tree in Senegal. It is slightly tangy and often sweetened, providing a unique and refreshing flavor profile that's both nutritious and delicious.

Bissap

Bissap, or hibiscus juice, is a beloved Senegalese drink crafted from hibiscus flowers. It is often sweetened with sugar and served cold, delivering a delightful combination of tart and sweet notes.
